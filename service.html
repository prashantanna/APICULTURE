Our Services





Honey Production

We rent out our bees to local farmers to pollinate eleven different food crops: Apple, blueberries, currants, strawberries , blackberries, rasberries, cranberries, kiwi, pumpkin, zucchini and squash. Bees are rented for the entire bloom cycle which is typically four weeks long. This process produces large amount of honey every cycle.


Bee-keeping Classes

We are committed to use our expertise to help the honeybees, which we do through our wide variety of courses and workshops. We offer beekeeping courses so you can begin doing it yourself. Courses and workshops are offered throughout the year.


Swarm Removal

We have a team of beekeepers on call ti remove bee swarms from inside or outside of your home or business. We use enviromentally friendly methods to physically remove the bees, leaving little to no damage to the surroundings.
